Our Story

15 Years of
Crafting Beautiful Spaces

From a small tile showroom in 2011 to India's most trusted tile and bath destination — our journey has been paved with passion, craftsmanship, and an uncompromising eye for quality.

Who We Are

Transforming Indian Homes Since 2011

TileHaven began with a simple belief: every Indian home deserves world-class tiles and sanitaryware at accessible prices. Today, we curate over 500 tile designs and 300+ bath products from top manufacturers in Italy, Spain, and India.

Our team of certified designers and product specialists work tirelessly to bring you the finest in floor tiles, wall tiles, sanitaryware, and bath fittings — whether you're renovating a single bathroom or designing a luxury hotel.
